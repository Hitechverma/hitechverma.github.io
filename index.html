Vigya ITI

Arya College of Engineering and Information Technology a premier institute in Professional Education with an aim to groom our young people to the height of technical excellence was established in year 2000. With this aim college has grown to significant heights, which has been witnessed by the numerous rewards and recognitions it has been honored in the past years. The growth of the college has been in all fields and a large number of students who have entrusted us. They are now found all around the world excelling in the fields of Engineering and Profession. Arya College Jaipur is imparting quality Professional Education to the youth with an objective to develop them to lead the global professional. Proud Aryans can be found in all walks of life with a niche edge.
